Get to Know This Modern Unidersity

Digital Age University is a private, non-sectarian university that takes pride on its E-sports academic programs, and game design and development. Founded on May 28, 2020 by Aiana Kamil Rubio is the first and only University in the Philippines and in Asia that offers e-sports, gaming design and development and is one of the world's largest e-sports universities in terms of enrollment found on one campus. The university is composed of only 3 main faculties, each conferring undergraduate, and graduate degrees. These degree programs have been accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).
